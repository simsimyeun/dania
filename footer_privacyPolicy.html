    • Visitor's IP address
    • Operating system
    • Browser type and version number
    • Web pages viewed

    This information and data is aggregated with all other visitor information for tailoring the website to customer preferences.

    For customers to register with an account on our Company website, the minimum information necessary to setup an account is limited to:
