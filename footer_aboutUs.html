About Us

Two Modern Brands, One Great Company

With a focus on quality furniture crafted for every budget, sister-brands Scandinavian Designs | Dania Furniture are modern purveyors of a global network of modern furnishings. The names Scandinavian Designs and Dania Furniture represent a unified brand that offers the same wide variety of pieces that suit every style.

We Make it Simple, You Make it Home

We've distilled the timeless Scandinavian concept of home into a brand based in simplicity and style. Our furniture showcases clean, minimal lines and multi-functional designs that organically fill any space with the feelings of light and warmth. Turning a house into a home is as simple as choosing from the pieces we've hand-selected to prioritize timeless style and your continued comfort.

HYGGE | The Scandinavian concept of coziness created when enjoying the simple, useful and beautiful elements that surround you.

A Scandinavian-inspired lifestyle: the concept of hygge(hue-guh) is at the core of Scandinavian Designs | Dania Furniture. Our pieces are intended to spark contentment, comfort and relaxation while infusing any space with the feeling of warmth and light. Through each hand-selected item we strive to blend the timeless tradition of Scandinavian design with your individual style. Our product is made to complement the practice of living well - because Scandinavian design isn't simply a style; it's a way of life centered around modern, artful simplicity.

Green Initiatives

Our goal has always been to help you turn your house into a home; one of the ways we are working to achieve this goal is to offer an assortment of eco-friendly green products. From pieces made locally in the United States using renewable resources to those crafted in Italy through cutting-edge sustainable processes, we have worked to bring you products that are responsibly crafted and healthy for our planet.

Modern Style For Every Budget

Since 1963 our objective has been to help every customer cultivate a welcoming atmosphere that goes hand in hand with the very ethos of home. At Scandinavian Designs, our assortment is rich with modern styles that fit every budget; we've perfected the art of contentment by making it simple for anyone to easily create a pleasant, comfortable lifestyle.

QUALITY | We consider ourselves to be experts in quality; from generations of our family to generations of yours, fine craftsmanship endures.

Quality is at the heart of all we do. We pride ourselves on offering an assortment of furniture pieces ready to stand the test of time. The quality of our work extends beyond our furniture - the foundation and history of our family business is based on valuing authenticity and honoring our obligations. Our entire team embraces these principles and strives to deliver the best customer service and the finest quality products at an exceptional value.
