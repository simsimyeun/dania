Shipping Information

We are only able to ship products when available in your nearest Distribution Center (DC). When you input your zip or postal code into our tracker, it allows us to see which items are available to reach you from your nearest DC. Some products will be ineligible to ship to your location based on availability in your nearest DC. We are not able to ship items from a split shipment, which includes products available in your nearest DC and a further DC. Some items may become available in your DC over time.

  • To see shipping options, just add the items you'd like to purchase to your cart. Once items have been added, go to your cart and enter your zip code in the Shipping Rates Calculator to see the shipping rates available in your location.
  • Orders consisting only of small parcels (lighting, accessories, etc.) most often ship within 1 week of placing the order.
  • Shipping time for furniture orders sometimes depends on your location. When you place your order, our Customer Care team will contact you to finalize delivery arrangements.
