FAQ

GENERAL

  • Who is Dania Furniture?

    View our ABOUT US page to learn all about Dania Furniture.

  • How do I contact Dania Furniture?

    The best way to contact us is to fill out the form on our CONTACT US page.

  • Does Dania Furniture have brick-and-mortar stores I can visit?

    Yes we do! Dania Furniture, along with our sister-brand Scandinavian Designs, has several locations in Arizona, California, Colorado, Idaho, Illinois, Minnesota, Nevada, Oregon, South Dakota and Washington. Click HERE to view a complete list of our showrooms and find the store closest to you.

  • What is your privacy policy?

    View our PRIVACY POLICY for details.

  • How do I get on the mailing list?

    Insert your email under CONNECT at the bottom of any page on our website to sign up for our email list.

SHOPPING

  • Do I need to register for an account to make a purchase?

    No, you do not need to register for an account to make a purchase. You can place your order without creating an account.

  • How may I pay for my purchase?

    Dania Furniture accepts Visa, Discover, Mastercard, American Express and Google Payments. We also offer easy-to-use financing options (see below).

  • Do you offer financing?

    At Dania Furniture, you can buy now and pay later! We offer financing through Wells Fargo (in stores) and Affirm (online).

  • When will my credit card be charged?

    Your credit card will be charged immediately upon placing your order on our site. Once your order has been placed, you will receive a confirmation message on your screen and via email.

  • How can I make changes to my order?

    Once an order has been shipped, please refer to our RETURNS & EXCHANGES policy. If you order has not yet shipped, please use our CONTACT US form for assistance.

  • How do I check the status of my order?

    All tracking details will be emailed to you upon placing your order. If you do not receive an email with tracking details, contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form for assistance.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    If your order has not yet shipped, please contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form to determine if your order can be cancelled. If your order has shipped, please refer to our RETURNS & EXCHANGES policy.

  • What is your return and exchange policy?

    View our RETURNS & EXCHANGES policy for details.

DELIVERY

  • What are your delivery charges?

    View SHIPPING INFORMATION for details.

  • How will I know that Dania Furniture received my order and my order has been placed?

    Immediately upon placing your order you will receive an email confirmation. Our Customer Care team will personally contact you to schedule delivery when your order ships.

  • How can I track my order?

    Tracking details will be emailed to you upon shipment of your order for FedEx. Freight and in-home delivery information will be provided by our Customer Care team. For further information or questions, please contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form for assistance.

  • Will all of the items in my order be delivered at the same time?

    We do our best to make sure that all items in an order arrive at the same time. If this is not possible, Dania Furniture Customer Care will contact you to let you know in advance.

  • How long will it take to get my shipment?

    Once you place your order online, items generally ship within a week. Specifics on shipping timeframes, along with tracking information, will be emailed directly to you once your order is processed.

  • How will I be notified of my delivery day and time?

    All tracking details will be emailed to you upon placing your order. If you do not receive an email with tracking details, contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form for assistance.

  • What do I do if an item arrives damaged?

    When your order is being delivered, you should inspect all items in your shipment before signing for the delivery. Upon inspection, if you notice something faulty or missing but the item is still usable, please keep the item and contact Customer Care immediately to arrange for replacement part, a scheduled repair or exchange. If the item is unusable, you should refuse the delivery. Please photograph the items that are damaged to help expedite the process.

  • Do you deliver everywhere?

    We ship to the contiguous United States. At this time, we do not ship to Alaska and Hawaii or any United States territories.

MERCHANDISE

  • What is your merchandise warranty?

    View our RETURNS & EXCHANGES policy for details.

  • How do I find assembly instructions?

    Contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form for assistance if your product did not include instructions or if you need a replacement copy.

  • What if I am missing parts for my item?

    Contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form to inquire about missing or replacement parts.

  • Do your upholstered items contain flame retardants?

    As of 2014, the state of California no longer allowed upholstered items to contain flame retardants. For all of our products manufactured after the 2014 effective date, we adopted this regulation company-wide in every state where we do business. All of our products are compliant with the California regulation.

  • How do I find furniture dimensions?

    All product dimensions are listed on the individual product pages under DIMENSIONS. If you have questions regarding the dimensions of a product, please contact Customer Care via our CONTACT US form for assistance.
